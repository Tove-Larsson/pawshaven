Our dogs

Choosing to adopt from a dog shelter like Paws Haven is a decision filled with compassion and positive impact. By providing a home for a dog in need, you become a hero in their story, offering a second chance and a forever family. Shelter dogs, each with a unique tale to tell, bring a special kind of magic to your life. The bond between a shelter dog and their adopter is extraordinary. Despite their past, these resilient creatures have an incredible capacity for love and gratitude. The joy they bring into your life is immeasurable, and the loyalty they offer is unwavering. You'll find a friend who stands by you through thick and thin, offering comfort with a wagging tail and a heart full of love. To be able to adopt you will first have to contact us.

This is Benny! Benny loves long walks, belly rubs, and sharing quiet moments with a gentle soul. Despite his past, Benny has embraced life with a joyful spirit, and his presence is a beacon of warmth in the shelter. Could you be the one to open your heart and home to Benny?

This is Frida! Frida loves fetching balls, exploring the outdoors, and showering her human friends with affectionate kisses.

This is Link! Link loves the couch and is not a morning dog, Link will often procastinate getting up in the morning. He loves chicken and to take a bath. has a fascination for collecting the softest blankets and arranging them in his bed just the way he likes!

This is Zelda! Zelda has a secret talent for juggling squeaky toys. Yes, you read that right! You might catch him entertaining himself with a squeaky symphony that echoes through the shelter halls.
